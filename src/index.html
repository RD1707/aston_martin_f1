The

AMR23

The Aston Martin AMR23 is a Formula One racing car designed and developed to compete in the 2023 Formula One World Championship. This machine represents the pinnacle of aerodynamic engineering and cutting-edge hybrid technology, a testament to the team's relentless pursuit of performance.

Engineering

The development of the AMR23 was a masterclass in precision and innovation. The team's engineers meticulously sculpted every surface to optimize airflow, working in synergy with the power unit integration to create a balanced and competitive package. The chassis, a monocoque of carbon fibre and honeycomb composite, provides a lightweight yet incredibly strong safety cell for the driver.

Advanced simulation tools and wind tunnel testing were crucial in validating the car's aerodynamic concept. The result is a car that is not only fast but also a platform for continuous development throughout the demanding F1 season.
